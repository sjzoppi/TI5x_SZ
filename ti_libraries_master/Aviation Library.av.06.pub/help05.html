AV-05 — Atmosphere, Speed, Temparature, and Altitude

This program calculates atmospheric conditions given a known pressure altitude. If the aircraft recovery coefficient, calibrated airspeed, and indicated temperature are also known, then the program will calculate the mach number, true airspeed, true air temperature, and the density altitude. The calculations are limited to altitudes under 36,039 feet.

PALT= pressure altitude (feet)
REC= aircraft recovery coefficient
REC= aircraft recovery coefficient

TAT(°C)= true air temperature (°C)
CAS= calibrated airspeed (knots)
TAS= true airspeed (knots)
M= mach number
DALT= density altitude (feet)
