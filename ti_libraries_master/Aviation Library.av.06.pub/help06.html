AV-06 — Predicting Freezing Levels, Lowest Usable Flight Level

This program contains two routines which operate independently:

Predicting Freezing Levels

This routine predicts the freezing level in feet above mean sea level, given a known temperature (°F or °C) and altitude (feet above MSL). The freezing level both in clear weather and in clouds is predicted.

Lowest Usable Flight Level

This routine computes the lowest usable flight level (LUFL) given the altimeter setting (ASET).

ALT= altitude in feet
DFzL= dry freezing level (clear weather)
WFzL= wet freezing level (clouds)
ASET= altimeter setting
LUFL= lowest usable flight level
