EE-10 â€” Chained Multiplication of Polynomials

This program performs the multiplication of two polynomials P1 x P2 = P3. The polynomials (P) are of the form a0 + a1x + a2x2 + ... + anxn where the coefficients are real numbers. The resulting product P3 may subsequently be multiplied by another polynomial. This chained multiplication process (P1 x P2 x P3 x ... x Pk) = Pproduct can be repeated until the limits of the calculator are reached. The degree of the final product polynomial must be ≤ 40. The intermediate products may be output, if desired.

  • Do either:
    • Printer used, press  D 
    • Printer not used, press  2nd   D' 
  • Initialize program :  A 
  • Enter the coefficients of P1 starting with a0, press  R/S , Repeat for all coefficients.
  • Prepare to multiply, press  B 
  • Enter the coefficients of P2 starting with a0, press  R/S , Repeat for all coefficients.
  • Output product with printer, press  C 
  • Output product without printer, press  C 

    a0 is displayed, press  R/S  to display a2 and repeat for a3 ... an.

  • Exit program, press  2nd   A' 

Registers: 00-07, 08-48 (coefficients of product), 49-89 (coefficients of multiplicand)
