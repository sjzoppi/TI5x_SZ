This programs converts a two-element series impedance network to an equivalent two-element parallel impedance network, or vice versa. The conversion is illustrated by the following diagram where Rs, Xs, Rp, and Xp refer to the series resistance, series reactance, parallel resistance, and parallel reactance in ohms, respectively.

Series Network

Parallel Network

Any two-element impedance network can be represented equivalently as a series network or a parallel network.

  • Initialize program :  SBR   CLR 

    Convert series to parallel

  • Enter the following in any order:
    • Series resistance Rs in ohms, press  A 
    • Series reactance Xs in ohms, press  B 
  • Compute the following in any order:
    • Parallel resistance Rp in ohms, press  E   C 
    • Parallele reactance Xp in ohms, press  E   D 

    Convert parallel to series

  • Enter the following in any order:
    • Parallel resistance Rp in ohms, press  C 
    • Parallel reactance Xp in ohms, press  D 
  • Compute the following in any order:
    • Series resistance Rs in ohms, press  E   A 
    • Series reactance Xs in ohms, press  E   B 

Registers: 10-13
