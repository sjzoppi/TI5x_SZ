This program computes the necessary component values for use in the design of active lowpass, highpass, and bandpass filters as illustrated.

Active Lowpass Filter

Given the peaking factor, the passband voltage gain in dB, the cutoff frequency in Hz, and C1 in farads, the program calculates values of C, R1, R2, and R3.

Active Highpass Filter

Given the peaking factor, the passband voltage gain in dB, the cutoff frequency in Hz, and C2 in farads, the program calculates values of C, R1, and R2.

Active Bandpass Filter

Given the 3-dB bandwidth in Hz, the midband voltage gain in dB, the center frequency in Hz, and C1 and C2 in farads, the program computes values for R1, R2, and R3.

  • Initialize program :  SBR   CLR 

    Enter the following as needed

    • peaking factor α (LP, HP), n, press  A 
    • passband voltage gain A (LP, HP) or midband voltage gain (BP) in dB, press  B 
    • cutoff frequency F (LP, HP) or center frenquency (BP) in hertz, press  C 
    • 3-dB bandwidth B (BP) in hertz, press  D 
    • C1 for (BP, LP) in farads, press  2nd   A' 
    • C2 for (BP, HP) in farads, press  2nd   B' 

    Compute component values:

  • Bandpass, to display

    R1 press  2nd   C' 

    R2 press  R/S 

    R3 press  R/S 

  • Lowpass, to display

    C press  2nd   D' 

    R2 press  R/S 

    R1 press  R/S 

    R3 press  R/S 

  • Highpass, to display

    C press  2nd   E' 

    R1 press  R/S 

    R2 press  R/S 

Registers: 10-18
