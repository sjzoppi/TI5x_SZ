This program allows you to play the game of Avey-Deucy against the calculator. The way the game is played is that the calculator first generates two random numbers and shows them to you. Then after you are given the odds, you enter your bet and a third random number is generated. If it is between the first two you win the bet. Your initial bankroll of $100 is updated after each hand.

  • Initialize program :  SBR   CLR 
  • Enter a seed number between 0 and 199017 and press  A . Your initial bankroll of $100 is displayed.
  • Press  B  to generate the first random number.
  • Press  C  to generate the second random number.
  • Press  E  to calculate odds.
  • Enter your bet and press  2nd   A' . If your win, the amount of your winnings appears in the display. If you loose, the negative amount of your bet appears in the display.
  • To see the number generated in step above, press  2nd   B' .
  • To display the value of your bankroll, press  2nd   C' .
  • To continue playing, start above where the first number is generated.
