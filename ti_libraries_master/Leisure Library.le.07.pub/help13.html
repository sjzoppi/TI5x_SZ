The game follows the rules of the familiar dice game:

  1. First roll of 7 or 11 wins
  2. First roll of 2, 3, 12 loses
  3. Any other first roll is your point and you win if it is rolled again before 7 is rolled (making your point). If 7 is rolled before your point, you lose.

The dice are represented on the display and printer as M.N where M and N are the numbers of the dice. All bets are even odds (1 to 1) and are made by entering the amount of the previous bet (0 for the first roll). Your initial bankroll is $100, and is updated after each round. You may continue playing until your bankroll is depleted ($0.00).

  • Initialize program :  SBR   CLR 
  • Press  . , then key any number and press  E . 100.00 will be displayed.
  • Key in your bet and press  C . This number will be displayed and printed. If you enter a bet larger than your bankroll, the display will flash 9's. You may press CE and enter a correct amount to continue.
  • Press  A  for your first roll which will be displayed unless you roll 2, 3, 12, 7 or 11 causing only your updated bankroll to be displayed. You may see what the roll was by pressing  D . If you won or lost on the first roll, start again with step above. Otherwise continue with following step.
  • Press  A  for your next roll, which will be displayed unless it is 7 or your point. In either if these cases, your updated bankroll will be displayed.
  • Repeat step above until you will or lose.
  • Continue until your $100 is depleted.
