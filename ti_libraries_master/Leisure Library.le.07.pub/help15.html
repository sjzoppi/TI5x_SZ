This game resembles the classic game of Hi-Lo; but only up to a point. This program sometimes "jives" you.

To play the game, you try to guess a number that the program has generated. It then tells you whether your guess is high, low, or correct. However, at the beginning of the game you have to enter a "probability of truth"", a number between 0 to 100. This entry determine how often the program "jives" you.

  • Initialize program :  SBR   CLR 
  • Enter the "probability of truth" as a number between 0 and 100 and press  A 
  • Enter a seed number between 0 and 199017 and press  B . The program then generates an integer between 0 and 100.
  • Enter a guess (0 to 100) press  E . If your guess is too high, 1 is displayed; too low, -1 is displayed. If your guess is correct, the correct number is flashed in the display.
  • Press  D  to display how many guesses you have taken.
