This program converts slope distance between measured by an electronic distance measuring (EDM or DM) unit to horizontal distance at sea level and at the elevation of the EDM unit. Given the slope as an input parameter, the distance in elevation between the two stations is calculated, and may then be used in SY-14 to calculate the horizontal distance at any specified elevation.

  • Select degree mode, press  2nd   Deg 
  • Initialize, press  2nd   E' 
  • Enter slope distance, press  A 
  • Enter zenith angle, press  B 
  • Enter height of DM unit, press  C 
  • Enter reflector height, press  R/S 
  • Enter theodolite height, press  D 
  • Enter target height, press  R/S 
  • Enter DM unit elevation, press  E 
  • Compute Hz Dist (IEL), press  2nd   A' 
  • Compute Hz Dist (SL), press  2nd   B' 
  • Compute Δ Elev, press  2nd   C' 

Registers used: 01-11, 15 and 17
