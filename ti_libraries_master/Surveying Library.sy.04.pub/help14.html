This program converts slope distance measured by an electronic distance measuring (EDM or DM) unit to horizontal distance at sea level, at the EDM unit elevation and at any specified elevation, given the difference in elevation (Δ Elev) of the two stations. Included are corrections for EDM unit height and reflector height.

  • Select degree mode, press  2nd   Deg 
  • Initialize, press  2nd   E' 
  • Enter slope distance, press  A 
  • Enter DM unit elevation, press  B 
  • Enter reflector elevation, press  C 
  • Enter DM unit height, press  D 
  • Enter reflector height, press  E 
  • Compute Hz Dist (IEL), press  2nd   A' 
  • Compute Hz Dist (SL), press  2nd   B' 
  • Enter specified elevation and compute Hz Dist, press  2nd   C' 

Registers used: 01-03, 05, 06, 12, 17, 18
