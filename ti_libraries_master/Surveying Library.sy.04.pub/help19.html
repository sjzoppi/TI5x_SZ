SY-19 â€” Intersection of a Perpendicular from a Point to a Line

Given a base line defined by a point (base point), a bearing and quadrant, and an offset point, this program will calculate the point of intersection of the perpendicular from the offset point to the line, the distance along the perpendicular, and the distance from the base point to the intersection.

  • Select the degree mode, press  2nd   Deg 
  • Initialize, press  2nd   E' 
  • Enter NB (base point), press  A 
  • Enter EB (base point), press  A 
  • Enter NO (offset point), press  B 
  • Enter EO (offset point), press  B 
  • Enter Bearing (DD.MMSS), press  C 
  • Enter Quadrant, press  C 
  • Compute, NI (intersection), press  D 
  • Compute, EI (intersection), press  E 
  • Compute, DistOI, press  2nd   A' 
  • Compute, DistBI, press  2nd   B' 

Registers used: 01-12
