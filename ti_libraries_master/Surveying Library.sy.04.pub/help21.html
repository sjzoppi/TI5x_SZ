This program computes the amount of fill that can be taken from a borrow pit where the pit is described by a grid of triangles and rectangles, with the grid dimensions and depth at grid intersections providing the information to compute the volume available in each grid section and the accumulated volume from a succession of sections.

  • To initialize, press  2nd   E' 
  • For each prism:
    • Enter altitude or length, press  A 
    • Enter base or width, press  B 
    • Identify section
      • For a triangle, press  C 
      • For a rectangle, press  D 
    • Enter corner depth, press  E 
  • Optional, display for current section
    • Altitude or length, press  2nd   A' 
    • Base or width, press  2nd   B' 
    • Cumulative volume so far, press  2nd   C' 
    • Section volume, press  2nd   D' 

Registers used: 00-03, 05-07
