SY-24 — Triangle Solution (2)

Knowing certain combinations of the attributes (sides or angles) of a triangle, the unknown attributes and the area can be calculated.

Knowing ASA

  • Enter side a: a  A 
  • Enter angle B: b  B 
  • Enter angle C: c  C 

Then, compute angle A:  2nd  A´

Now, you can compute the remaining angles, in either order:

  • side b:  D 
  • side c:  E 

Knowing SAA

  • Enter side a: a  A 
  • Enter angle A: b  B 
  • Enter angle C: A  C 

Then, compute angle B:  2nd   B' .

Now, you can obtain the remaining angles, in either order:

  • side b:  D 
  • side c:  E 

To calculate Area,  2nd   C' 

Formulas Used

A + B + C = 180° or 2π

c2 = a2 + b2 - 2ab cos C

a ÷ sin A = b ÷ sin B = c ÷ sin C

Registers used: 01-06.
