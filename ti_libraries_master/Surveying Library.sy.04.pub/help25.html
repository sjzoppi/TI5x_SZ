For an arc of a circle, this program calculates the remaining parameters when certain pars are given. One of the following pairs of parameters must be supplied as input:

  • Radius and Center angle:
    • Enter R, press  A 
    • Enter Δ (DDD.MMSS), press  R/S 
  • Radius and Chord length:
    • Enter R, press  B 
    • Enter C, press  R/S 
  • Radius and Arc length:
    • Enter R, press  C 
    • Enter L, press  R/S 
  • Chord length and Central angle:
    • Enter C, press  D 
    • Enter Δ (DDD.MMSS), press  R/S 
  • Central angle and Arc lenght:
    • Enter Δ (DDD.MMSS), press  E 
    • Enter L, press  R/S 

Then, calculate the remaining values:

  • Central angle and Radius:
    • Δ, press  2nd   A' 
    • Radius, press  R/S 
  • Arc length and Chord length:
    • L, press  2nd   B' 
    • C, press  R/S 
  • Area and segment area:
    • A, press  2nd   C' 
    • a, press  R/S 

Registers used: 00-04.
